Borrowers already have a repayment strategy.

Borrowers with multiple debts choose repayment order based on interest rates, negotiation flexibility, and cash flow. Financial institutions are competing for repayment priority.

Current industry approach
  1. Delinquency occurs
  2. Then collections begin

But the real problem starts earlier.

◎
Interest rates
Borrowers prioritize loans that feel “expensive” or urgent.
◈
Negotiation flexibility
If terms can be renegotiated, repayment urgency decreases.
◍
Cash flow
